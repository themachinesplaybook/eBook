Appendix A: Tools and Resources

Important Disclaimer

The tools, apps, services, and resources listed in this appendix are provided for informational purposes only. I have no financial interest, affiliate relationships, or commercial arrangements with any of the products, services, or organisations mentioned. These recommendations are based solely on my personal research and experience during recovery.

Technology and services change frequently. Verify current availability, pricing, and features before use. What worked for me may not work for everyone. Always consult with qualified professionals for personalised advice.

Australian Gambling Support Services

National Helplines

Gambling Help Online

  • Phone: 1800 858 858 (24/7, free, confidential)
  • Online chat: www.gamblinghelponline.org.au
  • Free, confidential support for anyone affected by gambling

Gamblers Anonymous Australia

  • Website: www.gaaustralia.org.au
  • Support group meetings across Australia
  • Peer support from people in recovery

Financial Counselling Australia

  • Phone: 1800 007 007 (National Debt Helpline)
  • Free, confidential financial counselling
  • Help with debt, budgeting, and financial recovery

Lifeline

  • Phone: 13 11 14 (24/7)
  • Crisis support and suicide prevention
  • Available for anyone in distress

Beyond Blue

  • Phone: 1300 22 4636 (24/7)
  • Mental health support and information
  • Resources for depression and anxiety

Self-Exclusion Programs

BetStop (National Self-Exclusion Register)

  • Website: www.betstop.gov.au
  • Blocks online and phone wagering nationally
  • Free, voluntary, legally enforceable
  • Covers all Australian licensed online wagering providers

Venue-Based Self-Exclusion

Each state and territory operates self-exclusion programs for physical venues (clubs, pubs, casinos). Contact your state's gaming authority or ask at any venue for information on how to register.

Blocking Software

These tools block access to gambling websites and apps across your devices:

Gamban (www.gamban.com)

  • Blocks all gambling sites and apps across all devices
  • Difficult to bypass or uninstall
  • Paid subscription (approximately $25/year)
  • Available for computers, phones, tablets
  • Covers thousands of gambling sites internationally

BetBlocker (www.betblocker.org)

  • Free, open-source gambling blocking
  • Blocks websites and apps
  • Good option if cost is a barrier
  • Available for multiple platforms

Cold Turkey (www.getcoldturkey.com)

  • General purpose website and app blocker
  • Create custom blocklists including gambling sites
  • Can set schedules for blocking
  • Can lock blocks so they can't be bypassed

Freedom (www.freedom.to)

  • Cross-platform app and website blocking
  • Session scheduling
  • Locked mode to prevent disabling

Tips for effective use:

  • Install on ALL devices, not just your primary phone
  • Set them up during a moment of clarity, not during urges
  • Tell your accountability partner when you've installed them
  • Use multiple blockers together for redundancy

Sobriety and Accountability Apps

I Am Sober (www.iamsober.com)

  • Track days since last gambled
  • Daily pledges and motivational messages
  • Community support features
  • Progress milestones and achievements

Nomo (www.nomoapp.org)

  • Sobriety clock showing time abstinent
  • Chip system based on recovery milestones
  • Private or social options

Easy Quit

  • Simple day counter
  • Health and financial benefits tracking

Mental Health and Mindfulness Apps

Headspace (www.headspace.com)

  • Guided meditations for various purposes
  • Specific content for managing cravings and urges

Calm (www.calm.com)

Location and Accountability Tools

Life360 (www.life360.com)

  • Family location sharing
  • Set up geofences around gambling venues
  • Alerts when entering high-risk areas

Google Maps Custom Lists

  • Create routes that avoid them
  • Free, built into Google Maps
