Appendix: Australian Gambling Support Resources

Disclaimer: The resources listed below are provided for informational purposes only. This book has no financial affiliation, partnership, or sponsorship arrangement with any of these organisations. They are included because they may help you. Always verify current contact details and service availability.

Where apps or services have costs, this is noted. I receive no benefit from any purchases or subscriptions.

National Services

Gambling Help Online

  • Phone: 1800 858 858 (24/7, free, confidential)
  • Website: www.gamblinghelponline.org.au
  • Live chat available on website
  • Free, confidential support for anyone affected by gambling
  • No affiliation with this book

Gamblers Anonymous Australia

  • Phone: 1300 888 711
  • Website: www.gaaustralia.org.au
  • Email: info@gaaustralia.org.au
  • Support group meetings across Australia (find local meetings on website)
  • Peer support from people in recovery
  • Completely free
  • No affiliation with this book

Financial Counselling Australia

  • Phone: 1800 007 007
  • Website: www.financialcounsellingaustralia.org.au
  • Free, confidential financial counselling
  • Help with debt, budgeting, and financial recovery
  • No affiliation with this book

Lifeline Australia

  • Phone: 13 11 14 (24/7 crisis support)
  • Website: www.lifeline.org.au
  • Text: 0477 13 11 14 (6pm-midnight AEDT, 7 days)
  • Crisis support and suicide prevention
  • Available for anyone in distress
  • No affiliation with this book

Beyond Blue

  • Phone: 1300 22 4636 (24/7)
  • Website: www.beyondblue.org.au
  • Online chat available on website (3pm-midnight AEDT, 7 days)
  • Mental health support and information
  • Resources for depression and anxiety
  • No affiliation with this book

State and Territory Services

New South Wales:

  • Gambling Help NSW: www.gamblinghelp.nsw.gov.au
  • Self-Exclusion: Liquor & Gaming NSW

Victoria:

  • Gambler's Help: www.gamblers help.com.au
  • YourPlay and BetStop programs
  • Phone: 1800 858 858

Queensland:

  • Gambling Help: 1800 858 858
  • Self-Exclusion: Office of Liquor and Gaming Regulation

South Australia:

  • Gambling Help: 1800 858 858
  • Statewide Self-Exclusion scheme

Western Australia:

  • Gambling Help: 1800 858 858
  • Problem Gambling Support Services

Tasmania:

  • Gambling Support Program: 1800 858 858
  • Self-Exclusion: Tasmanian Liquor and Gaming Commission

Australian Capital Territory:

  • Gambling Counselling Services: (02) 6205 4545
  • Problem Gambling Assistance Fund

Northern Territory:

  • Problem Gambling Services: 1800 858 858
  • Self-Exclusion: Licensing NT

Online Resources

BetStop (National Self-Exclusion Register)

  • Website: www.betstop.gov.au
  • Blocks online and phone wagering nationally
  • Free, voluntary, legally enforceable

ASIC MoneySmart

  • Website: www.moneysmart.gov.au
  • Financial guidance and resources
  • Budgeting tools and calculators

Gambling Research Australia

  • Fact sheets and research about gambling harm
  • Educational resources

Mobile Apps for Recovery

Blocking Apps:

Gamban (Blocking Software)

  • Website: www.gamban.com
  • Cost: Subscription required (approximately $3-4 USD/month or $30-40 USD/year)
  • Blocks gambling sites and apps across all devices
  • Available for iOS, Android, Windows, Mac
  • No affiliation with this book; included as a potential tool

BetBlocker (Blocking Software)

  • Website: www.betblocker.org
  • Cost: Completely free
  • Open-source gambling blocking software
  • Blocks gambling sites and apps
  • Available for multiple platforms
  • No affiliation with this book; included as a potential tool

Sobriety Tracking:

I Am Sober

  • Cost: Free with optional premium features
  • Tracks gambling-free days, motivation, and milestones
  • No affiliation with this book

Nomo - Sobriety Clocks

  • Cost: Free with optional premium features
  • Multiple sobriety trackers, community support
  • No affiliation with this book

Easy Quit

  • Cost: Free with optional premium features
  • Tracks progress and savings
  • No affiliation with this book

Mental Health:

Smiling Mind

  • Cost: Completely free
  • Australian-developed mindfulness and meditation app
  • Evidence-based programs
  • No affiliation with this book

Headspace

  • Cost: Subscription required (approximately $12-16 AUD/month)
  • Guided meditation and mindfulness
  • No affiliation with this book; included as a potential tool

Calm

  • Cost: Subscription required (approximately $15-20 AUD/month)
  • Meditation, sleep stories, relaxation
  • No affiliation with this book; included as a potential tool

Financial Tracking:

PocketBook (Australian)

  • Cost: Free
  • Budget tracking, bill reminders
  • No affiliation with this book

MoneyBrilliant (Australian)

  • Cost: Free basic version, premium features available
  • Personal finance management
  • No affiliation with this book

YNAB (You Need A Budget)

  • Cost: Subscription required (approximately $14-17 AUD/month)
  • Comprehensive budgeting tool
  • No affiliation with this book; included as a potential tool

Books and Further Reading

  • The Easy Way to Stop Gambling by Allen Carr
  • Overcoming Gambling Addiction by Alex Blaszczynski
  • Breaking Free from Compulsive Gambling by Mark Griffiths
  • Pathological Gambling: A Clinical Guide to Treatment (for professionals/serious students)

Emergency Contacts

If you or someone you know is in crisis:

  • Lifeline: 13 11 14
  • Suicide Call Back Service: 1300 659 467
  • Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636
  • 000 for emergencies

Remember

  • Help is available 24/7
  • All counseling is free and confidential
  • Recovery is possible
  • You don't have to do this alone
  • Reaching out for help is strength, not weakness

If you take nothing else from this book, take this: call 1800 858 858 and talk to someone today. Your life is worth more than the machines can ever offer.
